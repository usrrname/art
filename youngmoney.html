Young Money

Curated by Michael Ruiz

Future Gallery, Berlin. May 26, 2012.

In her work Chan explores Internet fetish communities.
Her focus is on the tension between mediated sexual fantasies and corporeal desires.
She contains her findings in creative gestures, through which she strives for a
better understanding of complex motivations and power structures.
Young Money is about the interconnected nature of various capitals: cultural, social, financial. 



 

With hypercontemporary found object installation and remix video,
this exhibition indulges in the valorization of youth and newness in both art and the internet.
Some like it hot, some like it sloshy. Debuting on www.intimatecrust.com,
Young Money is a remix video that divulges
Chan's critical fantasies of white masculinity in relation to the internet.
